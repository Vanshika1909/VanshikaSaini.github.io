IEEE IGDTUW

IEEE IGDTUW is a dynamic student branch dedicated to fostering technological innovation and professional growth among engineering students at IGDTUW. It provides a platform to connect, learn, and make a difference through impactful events and initiatives.

Being a part of IEEE IGDTUW has truly transformed my college journey. From a shy and timid first-year student, I evolved into a confident leader, learning to manage events and inspire creativity. I had the incredible opportunity to host inspiring tech influencers like Harkirat Singh and Nishant Chahar, which motivated me to aim higher in my own aspirations. The connections I made with seniors and peers have been invaluable, as we worked together to bring ideas to life and create lasting memories. Receiving the IEEE WIE Student Volunteer Award was a moment of immense pride, a testament to the dedication and passion I poured into this community. I will always cherish the camaraderie, the shared experiences, and the sense of belonging that IEEE IGDTUW has given me.
– Vanshika Saini, IEEE General Secretary
